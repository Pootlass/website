This is about me and my home town Bella Coola, BC

This is the sign you will see before entering Downtown Bella Coola. There is only one way in and one way out, when you drive into Bella Coola Valley.


Now, we have BC Ferries, coming to Bella Coola, now. Our BC Ferries busy In-season is Spring and Summer Seasons. Off-season, still operates, not much sails, during Autumn and Fall/Winter Season.


I was born and raised in my home town of Bella Coola, BC. Population, around 3,000 in valley. I am father 3, two boys and girl. Three grandchildren, two boys and girl :-)


I graduated from Acwsalcta School 1988, in Bella Coola. I was actually, one of the first graduates from Acwsalcta School, when it officially opened in mid or late 80's.

I have learned our traditional dances.
